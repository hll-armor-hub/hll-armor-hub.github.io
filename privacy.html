Overview

Hell Let Loose Armor Hub (hll-armor-hub.com) is a fan-made guide and tools site for Hell Let Loose players. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you visit the site, how third-party services we use may process data, and what choices you have.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described on this page. If you do not agree, please do not use the site.

Information we collect

We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect names, email addresses, or payment information directly through this site.

When you visit, the following may be collected automatically by us or by third-party services embedded on the site:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages viewed, referring URL, and general usage data
  • IP address (often processed in aggregated or anonymized form by analytics providers)
  • Theme and UI preferences stored locally in your browser (see below)

Cookies and local storage

This site may use cookies and similar technologies placed by third-party partners, including advertising and analytics providers.

We also store some preferences in your browser's local storage, such as your selected color theme and calculator settings. This data stays on your device and is used only to improve your experience on return visits. It is not sold or shared for advertising on its own.

You can clear cookies and local storage at any time through your browser settings. Doing so may reset site preferences.

Google AdSense and advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of third-party vendor cookies for personalized advertising at aboutads.info.

For more information on how Google uses data from sites that use its services, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Other third-party services

We use additional services that may collect limited technical data when you load pages or embedded content:

  • Cloudflare Web Analytics for privacy-oriented traffic measurement

  • Google Fonts and Font Awesome (CDN) to deliver typography and icons

Each third party operates under its own privacy policy. We do not control how those services handle data once you interact with their content or requests.

How we use information

Information processed through analytics and advertising tools is used to:

  • Understand how visitors use the site so we can improve guides and tools
  • Measure traffic and performance
  • Display and measure advertisements that help support the site
  • Maintain security and reliability

We do not sell your personal information.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for a general gaming audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this site, please contact us and we will work to remove it.
