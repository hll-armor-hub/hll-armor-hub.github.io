# General notes

3d live map instead of a 2d map
All 3 HQs are given default 150 supplies. Nodes are still a thing but this should reduce the annoyance of building them by a lot.
No jeeps
No halftracks
Only tank tier is medium (missing recon, light, heavy)
Boats are spawnable when the engine is off
Transport helicopters are spawnable when the engine is off
Supply helicopters are not spawnable when the engine is off
Helicopters have a height limit likely similar to admin cam height limit in WW2

Cannot prone jump
Crouch jump mostly works (slight delay?)
Crouch vaulting works
Prone vaulting works
Crouch/prone vaulting will work even on taller head-level objects you wouldn't expect to be able to vault over
Holding spacebar vaulting works similar but different. Can only vault once and then it stops. Cannot infinitely vault anymore
Truck drifting in neutral is back - but cannot go faster than max speed when engine is on. Drifting is silent
Vaulting is very generous. You can continuously vault up a rock wall you would have to go around normally in WW2. Imagine vaulting your way to the top of the Remagen mountain straight up the rocks

Supply trucks still drop 150 supplies
Supply helicopters drop ? supplies (expecting 150)
Could not get supply helicopter to drop supplies while flying solo. Logistics officer role (SL) could not drop supplies hovering mid air and switching seats. Is the only way to drop supplies the pilot role in the co-pilot seat? Supply amount was only shown in the co-pilot seat.
Vehicles take engine damage in water
Out of bounds timer (on foot) is 10 seconds
Out of bounds timer (vehicle) is 30 seconds (even helicopters)
You can go decently far out of bounds but there is an invisible blocking wall eventually
Cannot build out of bounds
Truck max speed 30 km/h
Truck holding handbrake (spacebar) no longer works to get into 5th gear while stationary
Boat max speed 21 knots
Truck/tank out of bounds 1 min 30 sec before destroyed
Out of bounds boats with the engine off are spawnable
Out of bounds boats do not destroy regardless if the engine is off or not
Out of bounds helicopters with the engine off are spawnable
Out of bounds helicopters do destroy in 1 min 30 sec

Exiting a helicopter will put you in the air and you'll take expected fall damage and likely die
Exiting a helicopter or switching seats such that all pilot seats are gone - the helicopter will drop and rotors will slow/stop

Garrisons still have a 200m build radius, 50 supplies in the red, 100 supplies in the blue
Garrisons cannot be placed in neutral territory
Outposts and NVA tunnels can be placed in neutral territory

NVA Tunnels have a 100m build radius
Tunnels cost no supplies
Tunnels have a placement cooldown of ~120 seconds
Tunnels have a connection radius of 500m
Tunnel networks can only have max 4 connections (5 locations total in a network)
15 total tunnels per team means 3 maxed networks of 5 tunnel exits each
Tunnel connections can be connected and disconnected instantly with no cost
You can travel to any tunnel in a network no matter how far or how many connections between
Distance travelled has no effect on "teleport" time - always takes only a couple seconds
Tunnels cannot be deployed on, have to walk over to one to use it

# Swimming

Double tap ctrl to dive underwater
Underwater takes ~6 seconds before drowning warning appears
Drowning timer takes ~6 seconds before instant death
Can spend ~11 seconds underwater if timing surfacing for air correctly
Vaulting while swimming to get out of the water works
